About

Psych Hack is a 36-hour hackathon aimed at encouraging student developers and problem solvers to create technology that addresses Mental Health and its multitudes while also making the world a better place to live. This is a plethora of opportunities for tech savvy individuals to flaunt their abilities, capitalise on new and old tech trends, and have their talent recognised and appreciated.

Tracks

Artificial intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Track is aimed at making you consider the vast oceans of different possible solutions to aid to the mental health and its repercussions

AR/VR

The Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality track aims at engaging students to dive deeper into these technologies and explore the vast possible solutions to tackle mental health related issues.

Mental Health

The Mental Health track is more of an open innovation track wherein you can let your imagination take the lead to build something wondrous revolving around our theme, Mental Health.

Education

The Education track focuses on empowering students, young and old, by building new ways to teach and learn (about mental health and self care). Especially as learning schedules changed during the pandemic, this is the prime time to innovate our current educational systems keeping in mind the mental conditions of students and helping them with it.

Blockchain

The Blockchain Track will help in reimagining the world and empower users to have full control and responsibility over their data. Use languages you already know to build something powerful which can provoke users in understanding the mental health revolving issues and tackling them.

Healthcare

The Healthcare track aims to create innovative technology that will help further our understanding of and provide solutions for health-related issues. This includes modern public health challenges such as the COVID-19 pandemic, mental health, nutrition and diet, and many other topics.

Self care

The Projects in the Self Care track should be focussed at helping the users know the importance of Mental Fitness and how to achieve that through self care.

Open innovation

The Open Innovation Track aims at letting your imagination take the lead to build something wondrous revolving around our theme, Mental Health.

FAQs

What is Psych Hack?

Psych Hack is a 36-hour hackathon aimed at encouraging student developers and problem solvers to create technology that addresses Mental Health and its multitudes while also making the world a better place to live.

What is a Hackathon ?

A hackathon, also known as a codefest, is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new software program.

Who can attend a Hackathon?

Anyone who is interested in technology can attend a hackathon to learn, build and share . Also , you should be of age 13 and above.

Can beginners participate?

Absolutely. We will have plenty of mentors and resources available,along with several workshops targeted for begginers. Come learn along and Experience your first hackathon with Psych Hack

Can we form Teams?

Most Definitely !! We Believe in Collaboration and encourage Hackers to work in a team of upto 2 to 4 members

Can I submit my previous project ?

We Apologize , but all hackathon's project should be developed during the event from scratch . We want honest conditions for all the hackers.Thats why all code should be done during the hackathon
